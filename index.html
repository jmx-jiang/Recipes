Chicken

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Salt 2g
Seasoning 10g

Step 1: Cut the chicken into 3-5 pieces.

Step 2: Place seasoning and salt and spread them on the chicken.

Step 3: Put the chicken on the stove for 10 minutes. Flip the chicken pieces when 5 minutes is up.

Rice

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 5g
Curry 10g

Step 1: Place curry on the stove for 15 minutes.

Step 2: While the curry is cooking, boil rice in the pot for 15 minutes.

Step 3: Place the curry on the rice once both are done.

Beef

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Seasoning 10 grams
Mayo 2 grams

Step 1: Place mayo, then seasoning on the beef.

Step 2: Cook the beef on the pan for 20 minutes.

Pasta

  • Time: 13 minutes
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Brown Butter 5 grams
Cheese Sause 5 grams

Step 1: Place brown butter and cheese sause in the bowl of pasta.

Step 2: Cook pasta for 13 minutes with the condiments.
